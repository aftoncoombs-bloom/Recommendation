# Client revenue projections


## Business objective

To generate regularly updated projections of client revenue growth. The projections should be generated as requested per client and in aggregate so as to better forecast client performance and ideally better identify potential high performing organizations that might greatly benefit from more attention.

## Completion

- Time series performance analysis (1) for all organizations and (2) speicific categories
- Generate projection models for all organizations and the specific categories, if applicable
- Provide an API endpoint to provide model outputs
